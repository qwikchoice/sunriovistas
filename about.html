About SunRio Vistas

A family-driven glamping experience rooted in learning, adventure, and connection with nature.

Our Story

SunRio Vistas was born from a simple belief: the best classroom is the great outdoors. Nestled near Folsom, California — along the winding American River — we offer premium RV glamping experiences that blend luxury comfort with hands-on discovery.

We created SunRio Vistas for families who want more than just a campsite. Every stay is thoughtfully designed to spark curiosity, build real skills, and create memories that last far longer than a weekend.

From assembling drones under open skies to meditating by the river to exploring the history of Folsom on foot — every experience here is intentional, meaningful, and fun.

🌿 Nature First

Everything we do is rooted in the natural world. Technology, learning, and creativity all thrive better outdoors.

🧠 Learning Always

We believe every moment is a chance to grow — whether it's science, culture, mindfulness, or entrepreneurship.

👨‍👩‍👧 Family at Heart

Our experiences are designed for all ages. We make sure every family member leaves with something meaningful.

🚀 Bold Adventure

We're not afraid to try new things — drone photography, AI workshops, laser engravers. Adventure takes many forms.
